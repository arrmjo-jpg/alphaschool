## Future Implications

If accepted: Assignments and Assessments — already existing, accepted entities (BUS-0011) — gain a real, reusable subjective-grading mechanism, and cross-assignment analytics ("are students improving on 'critical thinking' across the year") become possible for the first time. Without it, every teacher improvises scoring criteria per assignment with zero consistency or aggregate analytics.
